# Privacy Policy for Deckwise Flashcards

Effective date: July 13, 2026

Deckwise Flashcards ("Deckwise") is a flashcard study app. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use Deckwise, including the optional Deckwise Pro subscriptions and advertising shown to Free users.

## Information You Create in Deckwise

Deckwise lets you create decks and flashcards and records basic study activity, review history, reminder settings, and theme preferences. This information is stored locally on your device. Deckwise does not operate an account or cloud-sync service that receives or stores your flashcard content.

Deckwise does not require account registration or login and does not include cloud sync or AI features.

Deleting Deckwise may delete locally stored decks, cards, settings, and study history according to your device and backup settings. Restoring a Pro purchase does not restore locally deleted study content.

## Deckwise Pro Subscriptions

Deckwise offers optional Monthly and Yearly Deckwise Pro auto-renewable subscriptions through Apple's App Store. No free trial is offered.

Apple processes purchases, billing, renewals, cancellations, and refunds. Deckwise and its developer do not receive or store your payment card or bank account information.

Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled. You can view, manage, or cancel a subscription in your Apple ID subscription settings. Cancellation takes effect according to Apple's subscription terms.

Deckwise includes a Restore Purchases command. Restore Purchases asks Apple and RevenueCat to verify eligible past purchases associated with your App Store account and restore the Deckwise Pro entitlement. Restoring a purchase does not restore decks, cards, or other locally stored study data.

## RevenueCat

Deckwise uses RevenueCat to retrieve subscription products, validate App Store transactions, determine whether the `pro` entitlement is active, and support purchase restoration.

RevenueCat may process information needed to provide these services, including:

- An anonymous RevenueCat customer identifier
- App and device information
- Subscription product identifiers
- Purchase, renewal, expiration, and entitlement status
- App Store transaction and receipt information
- Technical, diagnostic, and service usage information related to subscription processing

Deckwise does not provide RevenueCat with your flashcard text or locally stored study history. RevenueCat processes information under its own privacy practices:

https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

## Advertising

The Free version of Deckwise uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements and frequency-limited interstitial advertisements after completed study sessions. Deckwise Pro removes both banner and interstitial advertisements while the Pro entitlement is active.

Google and its partners may collect or process information to provide, measure, secure, and improve advertising. This may include device information, advertising identifiers, app interactions, approximate location, advertising data, diagnostics, and performance information, subject to device settings, consent requirements, and applicable law.

Google's use of information from apps that use its services is described here:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Advertising controls are available here:

https://adssettings.google.com/

## Contact Support

Contact Support opens your email application. Sending an email is optional and user-initiated. The content of your message and any information included by your email provider are processed to respond to your request. Deckwise does not automatically upload your support message.

## Data Sharing

Deckwise does not sell your personal information. Locally created decks, cards, and study history are not shared with the developer because Deckwise does not operate a server that collects this content.

Subscription-related information may be processed by Apple and RevenueCat. Advertising-related information may be processed by Google AdMob and its partners as described above.

## Data Retention

Local study data remains on your device until you edit or delete it, delete the app, or device and backup behavior removes it. Apple, RevenueCat, Google, and your email provider retain information according to their respective policies and legal obligations.

## Children's Privacy

Deckwise is intended for general learning and productivity use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through Deckwise-owned servers. Apple, RevenueCat, Google AdMob, and other service providers process information according to their own policies, age requirements, and applicable law.

## Security

Deckwise stores study data locally on your device. Keep your device secure and use operating-system protections such as passcodes, biometrics, and backups where appropriate. Subscription transactions are processed through Apple and RevenueCat rather than through a Deckwise payment server.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when Deckwise features or third-party services change. The effective date at the top of this policy indicates the latest revision.
