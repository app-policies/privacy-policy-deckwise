# Privacy Policy for Deckwise Flashcards

Effective date: July 4, 2026

Deckwise Flashcards ("Deckwise") is a simple flashcard study app. This Privacy Policy explains how Deckwise handles information when you use the app.

## Information You Create in the App

Deckwise lets you create decks and flashcards, including the front and back text of each card. In Deckwise v1.0.0, this study content is stored locally on your device.

Deckwise v1.0.0 does not provide user accounts, login, cloud sync, or account-based storage. We do not operate a Deckwise server that receives or stores your flashcard content.

## No Login, Cloud Sync, AI, or Paid Account

Deckwise v1.0.0 does not include:

- Account registration or login
- Cloud sync
- AI features
- In-app purchases or subscriptions
- Paid account features

## Local Storage

Your decks, cards, and basic review information are stored on your device. If you delete the app, your locally stored Deckwise data may also be deleted according to your device operating system behavior.

## Advertising

Deckwise v1.0.0 uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements.

Google and its partners may collect or process information to provide, measure, and improve ads. This may include device information, advertising identifiers, app usage data, approximate location, diagnostics, and other information as described by Google.

Google's use of information from apps that use its services is described here:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

You can learn more about Google advertising controls here:

https://adssettings.google.com/

## Data Sharing

Deckwise does not sell your personal information. Deckwise does not share your locally created flashcard content with us because that content is stored on your device in v1.0.0.

Advertising-related data may be processed by Google AdMob and its partners as described above.

## Children's Privacy

Deckwise is intended for general learning and productivity use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through Deckwise-owned servers. Advertising providers may process data according to their own policies and applicable law.

## Security

Deckwise stores app data locally on your device. Please keep your device secure and use operating system protections such as passcodes, biometrics, and device backups where appropriate.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date above.
